Laser Engraving Shop

BUS Core gives laser engraving shops a structured way to track substrates, record production runs, and calculate real per-unit cost — without spreadsheets or cloud services.

Common Problems

  • Multiple substrate types (wood, acrylic, leather, metal blanks) tracked informally or not at all
  • Material waste varies by design, making accurate costing difficult
  • No record of which materials and quantities went into past batches
  • Inventory of blanks and substrates drifts without a system
  • Pricing repeat or custom orders becomes guesswork

Where BUS Core Fits

  • Define materials (substrates, blanks, consumables) once and track quantities as they move through production
  • Create production blueprints for repeatable engravings, pairing material inputs with finished outputs
  • Record each manufacturing run so you know exactly what was produced and what was consumed
  • Calculate real per-unit cost including material usage
  • Maintain a production ledger showing what moved through the shop and when

Practical Fit Checklist

  • You engrave physical products in batches
  • You manage multiple substrate types or blank inventories
  • You need to know actual material cost per product
  • You want a structured record of past production runs
  • You prefer a local-first tool that runs on your own machine

Privacy Policy

BUS Core — the digital shop jig for manufacturing workflows.

Application Privacy

BUS Core does not collect personal data.

The application runs on your machine with SQLite. No telemetry is transmitted. No usage analytics are embedded. No background data is sent to external servers.

The software does not require accounts, subscriptions, or remote authentication.

Website Analytics

The BUS Core website collects limited aggregate metrics, including page views and download counts.

These metrics are used solely to understand overall usage and release distribution volume.

No personal profiles are created. No tracking cookies are used for advertising. No data is sold or shared for marketing purposes.

Standard web server logs may include IP address and user agent as part of routine hosting operations.

Data Ownership

All business data created within BUS Core remains on your machine.

Users are responsible for their own backups and local data management.
